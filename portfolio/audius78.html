Audius 78

Spring 2016

For the final project of Olin.js, a full-stack javascript development course at Olin, my team of four created 'Audius78,' the world's first collaborative audio-editing software (we think). Similar to Google docs, multiple users can view in real time changes to uploaded audio clips, such as splicing, moving, fading in and out, etc.

This project utilized sockets, Node.js, React.js, and a material design frontend.

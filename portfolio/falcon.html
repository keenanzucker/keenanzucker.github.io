Falcon Camera Systems

Fall 2015

Falcon Camera Systems was a final project for Principles of Engineering. In a team of five, we developed a cable-suspended camera 'flying' system. We can mount our 'nodes', which are three ClearPath Servo motors with a complex spooling mechanism, onto the walls or ceiling of a room. Each node is controlled by an Arduino, which are all connected wirelessly via Bluetooth RN-41 modules t. We use a Python script to send commands. Similar idea to a football stadium with a 'Skycam,' Falcon is targetted for indoor footage and is modular for any room. Also, can replicate precise movement of gimbal for multiple shots of a scene.

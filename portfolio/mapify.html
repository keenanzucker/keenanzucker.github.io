Mapify

Spring 2016

For a weeklong webapp project for Olin.js, a full-stack javascript development course at Olin, my team of three created 'Mapify.' The assignment was to combine two APIs to create a new webapp. We combined Google Maps and Spotify to create an app that would take in the user's location, destination, and favorite artists to generate and add a custom playlist to the user's account lasting the duration of the trip. It also allowed the user to preview and listen to snippets of the playlist.
